# 小学四年级英语写作分级练习与高频词块（沪教版）

> **适用教材**：上海教育出版社（沪教版 / 上教版）牛津英语 · 义务教育教科书（教育部审定 2013）
> **年级**：四年级（全学年）
> **训练方法**：分阶段四步训练法 —— 写对单词 → 写对句子 → 写连句子 → 写小段落
> **本年级写作目标**：能围绕"一幅图"写出多句话，能用现在进行时、there be、方位介词描述场景，能写 4—5 句的小段落（我的周末 / 我最喜欢的季节 / 我的一天），做到句子完整、前后连贯、首字母大写、标点正确。

---

## 一、本年级写作能力目标

| 维度 | 三年级水平（起点） | 四年级目标（终点） |
|------|----------------|----------------|
| 词 | 会拼日常名词、动词原形 | 会写动词 ing 形式、第三人称单数 +s/es |
| 句 | 写对一句完整的话 | 用进行时 / there be / 方位句描述图片 |
| 句群 | 用 and / but 连两句 | 用 then / after that / first 等连接词排顺序 |
| 段 | 围绕主题写 2—3 句 | 看图写 4—5 句小段落，结构完整 |
| 语法 | 一般现在时、can | 现在进行时、there be、方位介词、三单、频率词 |
| 话题 | 日常、能力 | 季节天气、职业、时间、周末、旅行地点、点餐 |

> **一句话目标**：看到一幅图，能从"有什么 → 在哪里 → 谁在做什么 → 天气/感受"四个角度，写出 4—5 句连贯的话。

## 二、第 1 阶段 · 写对单词

> 本阶段重点：动词 **-ing** 形式、第三人称单数 **+s/es** 的拼写变化、分类与补全。这是后面写进行时句子和三单句子的"地基"。

### 练习 1-1：写出动词的 -ing 形式（10 题）

| 题号 | 动词原形 | 写出 -ing 形式 |
|------|----------|----------------|
| 1 | play | __________ |
| 2 | read | __________ |
| 3 | run | __________ |
| 4 | swim | __________ |
| 5 | make | __________ |
| 6 | write | __________ |
| 7 | sit | __________ |
| 8 | dance | __________ |
| 9 | watch | __________ |
| 10 | put | __________ |

**参考答案**：1. playing 2. reading 3. running 4. swimming 5. making 6. writing 7. sitting 8. dancing 9. watching 10. putting

> **拼写口诀**：一般直接加 ing；以不发音 e 结尾去 e 加 ing（make→making）；重读闭音节双写末字母再加 ing（run→running, sit→sitting, swim→swimming, put→putting）。

### 练习 1-2：写出动词的第三人称单数（10 题）

| 题号 | 动词原形 | 第三人称单数（he/she/it） |
|------|----------|----------------|
| 1 | like | __________ |
| 2 | go | __________ |
| 3 | watch | __________ |
| 4 | study | __________ |
| 5 | do | __________ |
| 6 | play | __________ |
| 7 | wash | __________ |
| 8 | have | __________ |
| 9 | fly | __________ |
| 10 | get | __________ |

**参考答案**：1. likes 2. goes 3. watches 4. studies 5. does 6. plays 7. washes 8. has 9. flies 10. gets

> **拼写口诀**：一般直接加 s；以 s/x/sh/ch/o 结尾加 es（goes, watches, washes, does）；辅音字母 + y 结尾，变 y 为 i 加 es（study→studies, fly→flies）；have 特殊变 has。

### 练习 1-3：单词分类（6 题）

把下列单词写到正确的类别里。

> spring｜doctor｜summer｜zoo｜teacher｜park｜winter｜museum｜farmer｜autumn｜nurse｜supermarket

| 类别 | 单词 |
|------|------|
| 季节 (seasons) | __________ |
| 职业 (jobs) | __________ |
| 地点 (places) | __________ |

**参考答案**：
- 季节：spring, summer, autumn, winter
- 职业：doctor, teacher, farmer, nurse
- 地点：zoo, park, museum, supermarket

## 三、第 2 阶段 · 写对句子

> 本阶段重点：连词成句、现在进行时与 there be 句型替换、选词填空、改错。目标是写出一句**主谓完整、标点正确**的话。

### 练习 2-1：连词成句（8 题）

把打乱的词整理成正确的句子，注意首字母大写和标点。

1. is / She / now / reading →
2. are / playing / They / football →
3. is / a / There / desk / room / the / in →
4. front / The / in / cat / the / is / of / box →
5. likes / He / very much / apples →
6. o'clock / It's / seven →
7. weekends / On / I / TV / watch →
8. between / sits / Tom / and / Sam / me →

**参考答案**：
1. She is reading now.
2. They are playing football.
3. There is a desk in the room.
4. The cat is in front of the box.
5. He likes apples very much.
6. It's seven o'clock.
7. On weekends I watch TV.
8. Tom sits between me and Sam.

### 练习 2-2：句型替换 —— 现在进行时（6 题）

照样子，把一般现在时改成现在进行时。
> 例：I read a book. → I **am reading** a book.

1. She sings a song. → She __________ a song.
2. They play games. → They __________ games.
3. He swims in the pool. → He __________ in the pool.
4. We make a cake. → We __________ a cake.
5. The bird flies. → The bird __________.
6. I write a letter. → I __________ a letter.

**参考答案**：1. is singing 2. are playing 3. is swimming 4. are making 5. is flying 6. am writing

### 练习 2-3：句型替换 —— there be（6 题）

照样子，根据括号里的词把 there is 改成 there are 或调整单复数。
> 例：There is a book on the desk. (three books) → There **are three books** on the desk.

1. There is an apple on the table. (some apples) → __________
2. There are four pens in the box. (one pen) → __________
3. There is a bird in the tree. (two birds) → __________
4. There is a girl in the room. (many girls) → __________
5. There are three cats. (a cat) → __________
6. There is a park near my home. (two parks) → __________

**参考答案**：
1. There are some apples on the table.
2. There is one pen in the box.
3. There are two birds in the tree.
4. There are many girls in the room.
5. There is a cat.
6. There are two parks near my home.

### 练习 2-4：选词填空（8 题）

从括号中选择正确的词填空。

1. There ______ (is / are) a museum near the park.
2. There ______ (is / are) three doctors in the hospital.
3. The ball is ______ (in front of / between) the two boxes.
4. Look! He ______ (swim / is swimming) now.
5. She ______ (like / likes) summer best.
6. It ______ (is / are) ten o'clock now.
7. My father ______ (always / am) gets up early.
8. The cat is ______ (next to / behind) the door. (它躲在门后面)

**参考答案**：1. is 2. are 3. between 4. is swimming 5. likes 6. is 7. always 8. behind

### 练习 2-5：改错（6 题）

每句有一处错误，请改正并重写整句。

1. She are reading a book.
2. There is two birds in the tree.
3. He like spring very much.
4. They is playing football.
5. it's eight o'clock.
6. The dog is in front the house.

**参考答案**：
1. She **is** reading a book.
2. There **are** two birds in the tree.
3. He **likes** spring very much.
4. They **are** playing football.
5. **It's** eight o'clock.（首字母大写）
6. The dog is in front **of** the house.

## 四、第 3 阶段 · 写连句子

> 本阶段重点：用连接词和时间词把句子串起来、问答转写、合并句子。目标是写出 2—3 句**前后有顺序、有逻辑**的话。

### 练习 3-1：用连接词 / 时间词连句（6 题）

用 **and / but / then / after that / first / on weekends** 填空，使句子通顺。

1. First I do my homework, ______ I watch TV.
2. I like summer, ______ my sister likes winter.
3. He gets up at seven ______ has breakfast.
4. We go to the zoo. ______ , we go to the park. (然后)
5. ______ I usually visit my grandparents. (周末)
6. She is singing ______ dancing now.

**参考答案**：1. then 2. but 3. and 4. After that 5. On weekends 6. and

### 练习 3-2：问答转写（6 题）

根据问句写出答句。

1. What are you doing? (我在读书) → __________
2. What's the weather like in spring? (温暖的 warm) → __________
3. What time is it? (8:30) → __________
4. What does he do? (他是老师) → __________
5. Where is the cat? (在桌子下面) → __________
6. What do you do on weekends? (我踢足球) → __________

**参考答案**：
1. I am reading a book.
2. It's warm in spring.
3. It's half past eight. （= It's eight thirty.）
4. He is a teacher.
5. The cat is under the table.
6. On weekends I play football.

### 练习 3-3：合并句子（6 题）

把两句话合并成一句。

1. There is a tree. There is a bird in the tree. → __________
2. He likes spring. He likes summer. → __________
3. I am reading. My brother is reading. → __________
4. The park is big. The park is beautiful. → __________
5. She is a nurse. She works in a hospital. → __________
6. It is sunny. It is warm today. → __________

**参考答案**（答案不唯一，给参考）：
1. There is a bird in the tree.
2. He likes spring and summer.
3. My brother and I are reading.
4. The park is big and beautiful.
5. She is a nurse and works in a hospital.
6. It is sunny and warm today.

### 练习 3-4：排序成段（5 题）

把下列句子按合理顺序排成一小段，写出序号。

> A. After that, we have a big lunch.
> B. On weekends, I get up at eight.
> C. First, I do my homework.
> D. Then I help my mother clean the room.
> E. In the afternoon, I play with my friends.

**参考答案**：B → C → D → A → E

## 五、第 4 阶段 · 写小段落（四年级重点）

> 本阶段重点：一图多句、看图写话，写出 4—5 句的小段落。掌握"**总—分—感受**"的简单结构。

### 看图写话的"四步支架"

| 步骤 | 问自己 | 常用句型 |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 看整体 | 这是什么地方 / 季节？ | It's a park. / It's spring. |
| 2 看有什么 | 有什么东西 / 人？ | There is / There are ... |
| 3 看在做什么 | 谁在哪里做什么？ | ... is/are doing ... / He likes ... |
| 4 写感受 | 你的心情怎样？ | I'm happy. / It's nice. |

### 范文 1：我的周末（My Weekend）

> **图片描述**：图中一个男孩星期六早上在房间里整理书包，下午在公园里和朋友踢足球，旁边有大树和长椅，天气晴朗。

**写作支架**：
- 时间：On Saturday / In the morning / In the afternoon
- 活动：clean my room / play football / read books
- 感受：I'm very happy.

**范文（5 句）**：
> On Saturday morning, I clean my room and do my homework. In the afternoon, I go to the park with my friends. We play football under the big tree. The weather is sunny and warm. I'm very happy on weekends.

### 范文 2：我最喜欢的季节（My Favourite Season）

> **图片描述**：图中是夏天的场景，太阳很大，孩子们在游泳池里游泳，有人在吃冰淇淋。

**写作支架**：
- 季节：My favourite season is summer.
- 天气：It's hot and sunny.
- 活动：go swimming / eat ice cream
- 感受：It's a happy season.

**范文（5 句）**：
> My favourite season is summer. It's hot and sunny in summer. I often go swimming with my father. I like eating ice cream, too. Summer is a happy season for me.

### 范文 3：我的一天（My Day）

> **图片描述**：四格图，分别是早上 7 点起床、8 点上学、12 点吃午饭、晚上 9 点睡觉。

**范文（5 句）**：
> I get up at seven o'clock. Then I go to school at half past seven. I have lunch at twelve. In the evening, I do my homework and watch TV. I go to bed at nine o'clock.

### 练习 4-1：看图写话 · 在动物园（5 题填空成段）

> **图片描述**：动物园里，有两只猴子在树上，一头大象在喝水，几个孩子站在大象前面看，天气晴朗。

用所给词把段落补充完整。

> (monkeys / elephant / in front of / sunny / happy)

It's a fine day at the zoo. There are two ____(1)____ in the tree. An ____(2)____ is drinking water. Some children are standing ____(3)____ the elephant. The weather is ____(4)____. We are very ____(5)____ today.

**参考答案**：1. monkeys 2. elephant 3. in front of 4. sunny 5. happy

### 练习 4-2：看图写段落（独立写作，4—5 句）

> **图片描述**：超市里，妈妈在推购物车，货架上有很多水果和牛奶，一个女孩拿着一个苹果，旁边有售货员。

**写作要求**：用 there is / there are 写有什么，用进行时写人在做什么，最后写一句感受。请写 4—5 句。

**参考范文**：
> We are at the supermarket. There are many apples and bottles of milk on the shelf. My mother is pushing the shopping cart. I am holding a big red apple. I love shopping with my mother.

### 练习 4-3：补全段落 · 描述天气与活动（5 空）

It's ____(1)____ (autumn 秋天). The weather is ____(2)____ (cool 凉爽的). The leaves are ____(3)____ (yellow). On weekends, my family ____(4)____ (go) to the park. We ____(5)____ (fly) kites together.

**参考答案**：1. autumn 2. cool 3. yellow 4. goes 5. fly
> 提示：第 4 空主语是 my family（看作整体，用 goes）；第 5 空主语 we 用原形 fly。

### 练习 4-4：自由写作题（任选一题，写 5 句以上）

1. **My Favourite Day**（我最喜欢的一天）：写你最喜欢哪一天、那天天气怎样、你做什么、心情如何。
2. **A Trip to the Park**（去公园）：写和谁去、公园里有什么、大家在做什么、感受。
3. **What Is My Mother?**（我妈妈的职业）：写妈妈的职业、在哪里工作、平时做什么、你的看法。

**评分提示**：5 句以上得满分；句子完整、首字母大写、标点正确每项加分；能正确用进行时或 there be 是亮点。

## 七、批改与评分要点

### 本年级评分看什么

| 等级 | 表现 |
|------|------|
| ⭐⭐⭐ 优秀 | 写够 4—5 句，能正确用进行时 / there be / 方位词，结构有顺序，标点大小写全对 |
| ⭐⭐ 良好 | 句子基本完整，能用 2—3 个目标句型，有少量大小写或单复数错误 |
| ⭐ 待加油 | 句子不完整或只有 1—2 句，句型单一，错误较多 |

### 批改三原则

1. **先表扬，后纠错**：先圈出写得好的句子和词块，再纠错，保护写作兴趣。
2. **一次抓 1—2 类错误**：本年级重点抓"is/are、动词三单 +s、进行时 be + doing"，不要满篇红。
3. **重点错误优先**：大小写、句号、is/are 一致、单复数、动词形式。

### 写作"五查"口诀

> 一查大写（句首、I）→ 二查句号问号 → 三查 be 动词（is/are 对不对）→ 四查动词形式（doing / +s）→ 五查意思连贯（句子有没有顺序）

### 本年级最常见的 5 个错误

| 错误示例 | 正确写法 | 错因 |
|----------|----------|------|
| She are reading. | She **is** reading. | 主谓不一致 |
| There is two cats. | There **are** two cats. | 复数用 are |
| He like summer. | He **likes** summer. | 三单漏 +s |
| I am play football. | I am **playing** football. | 进行时缺 -ing |
| in front the house | in front **of** the house | 方位短语缺 of |

---

> **教师寄语**：四年级是从"写句子"走向"写段落"的关键一年。坚持"一图多句"的训练，让孩子习惯从"有什么—在哪里—在做什么—感受"四个角度观察和表达，写作就会越来越顺。
