> **教材**：上海教育出版社（沪教版 / 上教版）牛津英语 · 义务教育教科书（教育部审定 2013）· **五年级**

## 二、第 1 阶段 · 写对单词

> 本阶段目标：过好"过去式关"和"比较级关"。动词过去式、形容词比较级是五年级写作的两大基石，拼错就会全篇时态混乱。

### 板块 1-1　规则动词过去式（加 -ed）

请写出下列动词的过去式。

1. play → ______
2. watch → ______
3. want → ______
4. visit → ______
5. clean → ______
6. help → ______
7. stay → ______
8. cook → ______

<details>
<summary>参考答案</summary>

1. played　2. watched　3. wanted　4. visited　5. cleaned　6. helped　7. stayed　8. cooked

**口诀**：一般直接加 **-ed**；以 e 结尾只加 **-d**（like→liked）；辅音+y 改 y 为 i 加 ed（study→studied）；重读闭音节双写末字母（stop→stopped）。
</details>

### 板块 1-2　不规则动词过去式（必背高频）

请写出下列动词的过去式。

1. go → ______
2. see → ______
3. have → ______
4. is / am → ______
5. are → ______
6. do → ______
7. eat → ______
8. buy → ______
9. take → ______
10. come → ______

<details>
<summary>参考答案</summary>

1. went　2. saw　3. had　4. was　5. were　6. did　7. ate　8. bought　9. took　10. came

**记忆提示**：日记三大金句动词 → **went**（去）、**saw**（看见）、**had**（有/吃）几乎天天用，务必写对。
</details>

### 板块 1-3　形容词比较级（加 -er / more）

请写出下列形容词的比较级。

1. tall → ______
2. big → ______
3. short → ______
4. long → ______
5. happy → ______
6. nice → ______
7. good → ______
8. beautiful → ______

<details>
<summary>参考答案</summary>

1. taller　2. bigger　3. shorter　4. longer　5. happier　6. nicer　7. **better**（不规则）　8. **more beautiful**（多音节用 more）

**口诀**：短词加 **-er**；good/bad 不规则（better/worse）；长词（beautiful, interesting）前加 **more**。
</details>

把下列单词填入正确的类别：*Spring Festival, taller, went, summer holiday, saw, stronger, birthday, had, bigger, Christmas*。

| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| ______ | ______ | ______ |

<details>

</details>

## 三、第 2 阶段 · 写对句子

> 本阶段目标：把过去式、比较级、be going to、情态动词放进完整句子里，做到**时态一致、语序正确**。

### 板块 2-1　连词成句（一般过去时）

把下列单词整理成正确的句子（注意首字母大写和标点）。

1. went / I / the / park / to / yesterday
2. saw / we / a / film / last / night
3. she / a / had / birthday / party
4. it / sunny / was / weekend / last
5. they / Beijing / visited / holiday / the / during

<details>
<summary>参考答案</summary>

1. I went to the park yesterday.
2. We saw a film last night.
3. She had a birthday party.
4. It was sunny last weekend.
5. They visited Beijing during the holiday.
</details>

### 板块 2-2　句型替换（用比较级 / be going to）

仿照例句改写。

**例**：Tom is tall. (Jack +) → **Jack is taller than Tom.**

1. The blue bag is big. (red bag +) → ______
2. Lily is young. (Anna +) → ______
3. I clean my room. (tomorrow, be going to) → ______
4. We visit the zoo. (next Sunday, be going to) → ______

<details>
<summary>参考答案</summary>

1. The red bag is bigger than the blue bag.
2. Anna is younger than Lily.
3. I am going to clean my room tomorrow.
4. We are going to visit the zoo next Sunday.
</details>

### 板块 2-3　时态填空（用括号动词的正确形式）

1. Last weekend I ______ (go) to my grandma's home.
2. Yesterday it ______ (be) rainy, so we ______ (stay) at home.
3. Look! She ______ (be) going to sing a song.
4. He ______ (be) taller than me now.
5. I ______ (see) a big elephant at the zoo last month.

<details>
<summary>参考答案</summary>

1. went　2. was; stayed　3. is　4. is　5. saw
</details>

### 板块 2-4　改错（每句一处错误）

1. I goed to school yesterday.
2. She is more tall than me.
3. We is going to have a party.
4. Last weekend I am happy.
5. He have a new bike last year.

<details>
<summary>参考答案</summary>

1. goed → **went**
2. more tall → **taller**
3. is → **are**
4. am → **was**
5. have → **had**
</details>

### 板块 2-5　情态动词填空（can / must / should）

1. You ______ wash your hands before dinner.（应该）
2. I ______ swim very well.（能够）
3. We ______ be quiet in the library.（必须）
4. You ______ go to bed early.（应该）
5. Birds ______ fly.（能够）

<details>
<summary>参考答案</summary>

1. should　2. can　3. must　4. should　5. can
</details>

## 四、第 3 阶段 · 写连句子

> 本阶段目标：用**时间顺序词**把几件过去发生的事串起来，学会合并句子，使段落连贯有条理。

### 板块 3-1　用时间顺序词写过去的事

用 **first / then / next / after that / finally** 把下面的活动连成一段话（一般过去时）。

提示动作：got up early → had breakfast → went to the park → played football → came home

1. ______（first）
2. ______（then）
3. ______（next）
4. ______（after that）
5. ______（finally）

<details>
<summary>参考答案（示例）</summary>

First, I got up early. Then I had breakfast. Next, I went to the park. After that, I played football with my friends. Finally, I came home happily.
</details>

### 板块 3-2　合并句子

用 *and / but / because / so* 把两句合并成一句。

1. I went to the zoo. I saw many animals.
2. The film was long. It was very interesting.
3. It rained yesterday. We didn't go out.
4. She likes summer. It is warm.

<details>
<summary>参考答案</summary>

1. I went to the zoo **and** saw many animals.
2. The film was long **but** (it was) very interesting.
3. It rained yesterday, **so** we didn't go out.
4. She likes summer **because** it is warm.
</details>

### 板块 3-3　问答转写（把回答写成陈述句）

根据问答，写出完整陈述句。

1. — Where did you go last weekend? — To the beach.
2. — What did you see? — A big ship.
3. — How was the weather? — Sunny and hot.
4. — What are you going to do tomorrow? — Visit my grandparents.

<details>
<summary>参考答案</summary>

1. Last weekend I went to the beach.
2. I saw a big ship.
3. The weather was sunny and hot.
4. I am going to visit my grandparents tomorrow.
</details>

### 板块 3-4　扩句（加细节）

在句子中加入括号要求的信息，让句子更具体。

1. I went to the park. （加：和谁 + 什么时候）
2. We saw a film. （加：什么样的电影）
3. She is my friend. （加：外貌/性格一个词）
4. It was a nice day. （加：天气怎样）

<details>
<summary>参考答案（示例）</summary>

1. I went to the park with my father last Sunday.
2. We saw an interesting cartoon film.
3. She is my kind and friendly friend.
4. It was a nice sunny day.
</details>

## 五、第 4 阶段 · 写小段落（五年级重点）

> 本阶段目标：能围绕一个话题写出 **5—6 句**、用过去时记事、有时间顺序和细节的小段落。下面三个板块分别练**写日记、写人、写景**，每个板块都配**写作支架**和**范文**。

### 板块 4-1　写日记（一般过去时）

**题目**：以 *My Weekend*（我的周末）为题，写一篇 5—6 句的日记。

**写作支架（照着填）**：
- 第 1 句（时间天气）：Last weekend it was ______ .
- 第 2 句（去了哪）：I went to ______ with ______ .
- 第 3 句（做了什么）：We ______ .
- 第 4 句（看到/吃到什么）：I saw / had ______ .
- 第 5 句（感受）：I was very ______ .
- 第 6 句（总结）：What a ______ weekend!

<details>
<summary>参考范文</summary>

**My Weekend**

Last weekend it was sunny and warm. I went to the park with my parents. We took many photos there. Then we had a big lunch in a restaurant. I saw a beautiful lake and some birds. I was very happy. What a wonderful weekend!
</details>

### 板块 4-2　写人（My Friend）

**题目**：介绍你的一位好朋友，写 5—6 句，描述外貌和性格。

**写作支架**：
- 第 1 句（是谁）：I have a good friend. Her/His name is ______ .
- 第 2 句（外貌）：She/He has ______ hair and ______ eyes.
- 第 3 句（高矮比较）：She/He is ______ than me.
- 第 4 句（性格）：She/He is ______ and ______ .
- 第 5 句（爱好）：She/He likes ______ .
- 第 6 句（感受）：I like her/him very much.

<details>
<summary>参考范文</summary>

**My Friend**

I have a good friend. Her name is Lucy. She has long black hair and big eyes. She is taller than me. She is kind and clever. She likes reading and drawing. We often play together. I like her very much.
</details>

### 板块 4-3　写景（假期 / 旅行）

**题目**：以 *My Holiday Trip*（我的假期旅行）为题，写 5—6 句，描述你去过的地方。

**写作支架**：
- 第 1 句（时间地点）：Last summer holiday I went to ______ .
- 第 2 句（怎么去）：I went there by ______ .
- 第 3 句（看到的景物）：It was beautiful. I saw ______ .
- 第 4 句（做的事）：I ______ there.
- 第 5 句（比较/评价）：It was ______ than my hometown.
- 第 6 句（感受）：I had a great time!

<details>
<summary>参考范文</summary>

**My Holiday Trip**

Last summer holiday I went to Hainan with my family. We went there by plane. It was beautiful. I saw the blue sea and white sand. I swam in the sea and ate a lot of seafood. The weather was hotter than in Shanghai. I had a great time!
</details>

### 板块 4-4　看图写话（图片用文字描述）

**图片内容**：一个小男孩在公园里。天气晴朗，有太阳。男孩在放风筝（fly a kite），旁边有一棵大树和一些花。地上有一只小狗在跑。（此事发生在上周日）

根据图片，用**一般过去时**写 5 句话。

<details>
<summary>参考范文</summary>

Last Sunday it was sunny. A boy was in the park. He flew a kite happily. There was a big tree and some flowers near him. A little dog ran on the grass. They had a good time.
</details>

### 板块 4-5　写未来计划（be going to）

**题目**：以 *My Plan for the Holiday*（我的假期计划）为题，写 5 句话，谈谈你打算做什么。

**写作支架**：The summer holiday is coming. I am going to ______ . I am also going to ______ . I want to ______ . It is going to be a happy holiday!

<details>
<summary>参考范文</summary>

**My Plan for the Holiday**

The summer holiday is coming. I am going to visit my grandparents in the countryside. I am also going to learn swimming. I want to read many storybooks too. My parents are going to take me to Beijing. It is going to be a happy holiday!
</details>

## 七、批改与评分要点

### 本年级评分看什么（5 分制）

| 分数 | 标准 |
| :---: | :--- |
| 5 分（优） | 5—6 句完整，**过去时正确**，有时间顺序词和细节，几乎无错 |
| 4 分（良） | 句子完整，时态基本正确，有 1—2 处小错（拼写/标点） |
| 3 分（中） | 能写 4 句以上，但时态混用（现在/过去），表达基本清楚 |
| 2 分（待提高） | 句子不完整或大量时态/拼写错误，意思勉强能懂 |
| 1 分 | 只能写零散单词，无法成句 |

### 批改三原则

1. **先看时态，再看细节**：五年级核心是过去时，先圈出所有动词，检查是否用了过去式（went / saw / was 等）。
2. **多鼓励，标亮点**：用波浪线划出写得好的词块（如 had a good time），写一句肯定的评语。
3. **错误归类不全改**：同类错误（如 goed）只在第一次出现处订正并讲清规则，培养自查能力。

### 五查口诀

> **一查时态**（动词是不是过去式 / be going to）
> **二查首尾**（首字母大写、句末标点）
> **三查主谓**（is/are/was/were 是否搭配）
> **四查比较**（taller / more 用对了吗）
> **五查连贯**（有没有 first/then/finally 等顺序词）

---

> 📌 **使用建议**：每周按"四阶段"循环训练一次——单词 5 分钟、句子 10 分钟、连句 10 分钟、段落 15 分钟。坚持一学期，过去时写作必能过关。
