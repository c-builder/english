# 六年级英语写作分级练习与高频词块

> **适用教材**：上海教育出版社（沪教版 / 上教版）牛津英语 · 义务教育教科书（教育部审定 2013）
> **年级**：六年级（小升初衔接阶段）
> **训练方法**：四阶段训练法 —— 写对单词 → 写对句子 → 写连句子 → 写小段落与应用文
> **本年级写作目标**：能围绕一个话题，运用**一般过去时、一般将来时、现在完成时**等多种时态，借助 `because / when / if` 等连词，写出**结构完整、6 句以上的成篇记叙文**，并能规范书写**日记、便条、书信/邮件、通知**等常见应用文。

---

## 一、本年级写作能力目标

| 维度 | 五年级基础 | 六年级目标（小升初要求） |
|------|------------|--------------------------|
| 时态 | 过去时、be going to | 过去/将来/现在完成时综合运用，时态前后一致 |
| 句子 | 写对单句、并列句 | 会写 `when / because / if` 复合句 |
| 篇章 | 写 3—6 句小段 | 写 **6 句以上成篇记叙文**，有开头—经过—结尾 |
| 文体 | 日记、写人写景 | 日记、便条、书信/邮件、通知 4 类应用文 |
| 衔接 | first / then / next | 增加 `first / after that / finally / however / so` |
| 表达 | 描述事实 | 会表达**观点与感受**（I think… / I feel…） |

## 二、第 1 阶段 · 写对单词

> 目标：写对各时态动词变化、比较级最高级、词性变化，为成篇写作打好"砖块"。

### 练习 1-A：写出动词的过去式（不规则为主）

写出下列动词的过去式（一般过去时）。

| 编号 | 动词原形 | 过去式 |
|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | go | ______ |
| 2 | have | ______ |
| 3 | see | ______ |
| 4 | buy | ______ |
| 5 | take | ______ |
| 6 | come | ______ |
| 7 | make | ______ |
| 8 | swim | ______ |

**参考答案：** 1. went　2. had　3. saw　4. bought　5. took　6. came　7. made　8. swam

---

### 练习 1-B：写出动词的过去分词（现在完成时用）

现在完成时结构为 `have / has + 过去分词`，写出过去分词。

1. eat → ______
2. do → ______
3. write → ______
4. read → ______
5. finish → ______
6. break → ______
7. lose → ______
8. give → ______

**参考答案：** 1. eaten　2. done　3. written　4. read　5. finished　6. broken　7. lost　8. given

---

### 练习 1-C：写出形容词/副词的比较级和最高级

按"原级 — 比较级 — 最高级"填空。

| 编号 | 原级 | 比较级 | 最高级 |
|------|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | tall | ______ | ______ |
| 2 | big | ______ | ______ |
| 3 | happy | ______ | ______ |
| 4 | good | ______ | ______ |
| 5 | beautiful | ______ | ______ |
| 6 | far | ______ | ______ |

**参考答案：**
1. taller / tallest　2. bigger / biggest　3. happier / happiest　4. better / best　5. more beautiful / most beautiful　6. farther(further) / farthest(furthest)

---

### 练习 1-D：词性变化（用所给词的正确形式）

1. He runs very ______. (quick)
2. She is the ______ girl in our class. (clever)
3. We should protect the ______. (environment 提示：直接用名词)
4. Reading is ______ than playing games. (interesting)
5. My mother is a good ______. (cook 提示：人)
6. They live ______ together. (happy)

**参考答案：** 1. quickly　2. cleverest　3. environment　4. more interesting　5. cook　6. happily

---

### 练习 1-E：将来时标志词与动词搭配

用 `will + 动词原形` 或 `be going to + 动词原形` 填空（按提示）。

1. Tomorrow I ______ (visit, will) my grandparents.
2. Look at the clouds! It ______ (rain, be going to).
3. Next year we ______ (move, will) to a new city.
4. This Sunday they ______ (have, be going to) a party.
5. I think the future ______ (be, will) better.

**参考答案：**
1. will visit　2. is going to rain　3. will move　4. are going to have　5. will be

## 三、第 2 阶段 · 写对句子

> 目标：在单句层面写对各种时态，标点、主谓一致到位。

### 练习 2-A：连词成句（注意大小写与标点）

1. went / I / the / to / zoo / yesterday
2. will / she / a / be / doctor / future / in / the
3. have / I / finished / homework / my / already
4. than / he / is / me / taller
5. going / we / are / clean / to / the / park
6. did / what / you / weekend / last / do / ?

**参考答案：**
1. I went to the zoo yesterday.
2. She will be a doctor in the future.
3. I have already finished my homework.
4. He is taller than me.
5. We are going to clean the park.
6. What did you do last weekend?

---

### 练习 2-B：句型替换（按时态改写）

按括号要求改写句子。

1. I clean my room every day. （改为一般过去时）
2. She watches TV. （改为一般将来时，用 will）
3. They play football. （改为现在完成时）
4. He is reading a book. （改为一般现在时）
5. We will go camping. （改为 be going to）

**参考答案：**
1. I cleaned my room yesterday.（每天改昨天）
2. She will watch TV.
3. They have played football.
4. He reads a book.（或 He reads books.）
5. We are going to go camping.

---

### 练习 2-C：时态综合填空（用动词正确形式）

1. Last summer, we ______ (go) to Beijing.
2. Listen! The birds ______ (sing) in the tree.
3. I ______ (already / finish) my project.
4. He usually ______ (get) up at six.
5. Tomorrow it ______ (be) sunny.
6. She ______ (not / see) this film before.
7. When I was five, I ______ (can) swim.

**参考答案：**
1. went　2. are singing　3. have already finished　4. gets　5. will be　6. has not seen (hasn't seen)　7. could

---

### 练习 2-D：改错（每句一处错误）

1. I have went to the museum.
2. She will goes to school tomorrow.
3. He is more taller than his brother.
4. Yesterday I go to the park.
5. They has cleaned the classroom.
6. We are going to visiting Hangzhou.

**参考答案：**
1. went → gone　2. goes → go　3. 去掉 more（或 taller→tall）　4. go → went　5. has → have　6. visiting → visit

## 四、第 3 阶段 · 写连句子

> 目标：用 `because / when / if` 等连词把句子连成连贯的段落。

### 练习 3-A：用 because 合并句子

用 `because` 把两句合并成一句。

1. I was happy. I got a present.
2. She stayed at home. It rained heavily.
3. We like Mr. Wang. He is kind.
4. He was tired. He ran a long way.
5. I love spring. The flowers are beautiful.

**参考答案：**
1. I was happy because I got a present.
2. She stayed at home because it rained heavily.
3. We like Mr. Wang because he is kind.
4. He was tired because he ran a long way.
5. I love spring because the flowers are beautiful.

---

### 练习 3-B：用 when 连句（注意时态一致）

用 `when` 合并句子。

1. I was five. I started school.
2. The phone rang. I was sleeping.
3. We saw a rainbow. We were on the hill.
4. She was young. She lived in the countryside.
5. It got dark. We went home.

**参考答案：**
1. I started school when I was five.
2. The phone rang when I was sleeping.
3. We saw a rainbow when we were on the hill.
4. When she was young, she lived in the countryside.
5. When it got dark, we went home.

---

### 练习 3-C：用 if 写条件句

用 `if` 完成句子（一般现在时表条件，主句用 will）。

1. If it ______ (be) fine tomorrow, we ______ (go) hiking.
2. If you ______ (study) hard, you ______ (pass) the exam.
3. If we ______ (save) water, the earth ______ (be) better.
4. If he ______ (not / hurry), he ______ (miss) the bus.
5. If I ______ (have) time, I ______ (help) you.

**参考答案：**
1. is / will go　2. study / will pass　3. save / will be　4. doesn't hurry / will miss　5. have / will help

---

### 练习 3-D：用连接词成段（排序+连接）

下面句子顺序被打乱，请用 `First / Then / After that / Finally` 重新排成一段通顺的话（写出正确顺序的句子）。

```
a. Then we set up our tents.
b. First we arrived at the campsite.
c. Finally we sat by the fire and sang songs.
d. After that we cooked dinner together.
```

**参考答案：**
First we arrived at the campsite. Then we set up our tents. After that we cooked dinner together. Finally we sat by the fire and sang songs.

---

### 练习 3-E：问答转写（把问答改写成陈述段落）

把下面问答改写成一段第三人称介绍。

```
Q: What's your hobby?      A: I like drawing.
Q: When do you draw?       A: I draw after school.
Q: Why do you like it?     A: Because it makes me relaxed.
```

**参考答案（示例）：**
His hobby is drawing. He draws after school. He likes it because it makes him relaxed.

## 五、第 4 阶段 · 写小段落与应用文（六年级重点）

> 目标：能写出结构完整、6 句以上的成篇记叙文，并规范完成 4 类应用文。每种文体配"写作支架"和完整范文。

### 5.1 成篇记叙文（≥ 6 句）

**写作支架（记叙文三段式）：**

| 结构 | 内容 | 常用句型 |
|------|------|----------|
| 开头 | 时间、地点、人物、事件 | Last … / One day … |
| 经过 | 按 First→Then→After that 顺序叙事 | First we … Then we … |
| 结尾 | 结果 + 感受/想法 | We were very happy. / It was an unforgettable day. |

**练习 4-A：**根据提示写一篇 6 句以上的记叙文，题目 *An Unforgettable Trip*（一次难忘的旅行）。
提示词：last summer / by train / the Great Wall / take photos / climb / tired but happy。

**参考范文：**

> **An Unforgettable Trip**
> Last summer, my family and I went to Beijing by train. We visited the Great Wall on the second day. First, we climbed up the wall slowly. Then we took many beautiful photos. After that, we bought some gifts for my friends. Although we were very tired, we felt really happy. It was an unforgettable trip, and I will never forget it.

---

### 5.2 看图作文

**练习 4-B（看图作文）：**

> **图片描述：** 图中是一个公园。早上，三个学生戴着手套，正在草地上捡垃圾。地上有一个垃圾袋。远处有树和长椅。天空晴朗，有几只小鸟。一块牌子上写着 "Keep our park clean!"

**写作支架：**地点 + 人物在做什么（现在进行时）+ 目的（环保）+ 感受/呼吁。

**参考范文：**

> Look at the picture. It is a sunny morning in the park. Three students are cleaning the park together. They are picking up the rubbish and putting it into a big bag. They want to keep the park clean and beautiful. The birds are singing in the trees. I think we should protect our environment, too. Let's keep our park clean!

---

### 5.3 日记（Diary）

**写作支架：**

```
顶部：星期 + 日期 + 天气（如 Sunday, May 5th    Sunny）
正文：用一般过去时记一天的事（First→Then→Finally）
结尾：今天的感受
```

**练习 4-C：**写一篇日记，记录"帮妈妈做家务"的一天。

**参考范文：**

> **Sunday, May 5th　　Sunny**
> Today I helped my mother do the housework. In the morning, I cleaned my room and washed the dishes. Then I went shopping with my mother. In the afternoon, we cooked dinner together. My mother said I was a good helper. I was a little tired, but I felt very happy. What a meaningful day!

---

### 5.4 便条（Note）

**写作支架：**称呼（如 Mum，）+ 正文（说清事由）+ 落款（姓名）。

**练习 4-D：**给妈妈写一张便条，告诉她你去同学家做小组作业，6 点回家。

**参考范文：**

> Mum,
> I have gone to Lily's home to do our group homework. We will finish it together. Don't worry about me. I will be back at six o'clock.
> 　　　　　　　　　　Lucy

---

### 5.5 书信 / 邮件（Letter / Email）

**写作支架：**

| 部分 | 内容 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 称呼 | Dear + 名字, | Dear Tom, |
| 开头 | 问候 / 写信目的 | How are you? I'm writing to tell you… |
| 正文 | 分享内容（计划/介绍/邀请） | I am going to… |
| 结尾 | 期待回信 | Please write back soon. |
| 落款 | Yours, + 名字 | Yours, Li Ming |

**练习 4-E：**给笔友 Tom 写一封邮件，介绍你的暑假计划（用 will / be going to）。

**参考范文：**

> Dear Tom,
> How are you? I'm writing to tell you about my summer plan.
> This summer, I am going to visit my grandparents in the countryside. I will help them on the farm and pick fruit. I am also going to read many books and learn to swim. I think it will be a wonderful summer.
> What are you going to do? Please write back soon.
> 　　　　　　　　　Yours,
> 　　　　　　　　　Li Ming

---

### 5.6 通知（Notice）

**写作支架：**标题 Notice + 时间/地点/事件 + 落款单位。

**练习 4-F：**为学校写一则通知：本周五下午 2 点在操场举行运动会，请同学们准时参加。

**参考范文：**

> **Notice**
> We are going to have a sports meeting this Friday afternoon at two o'clock on the playground. All the students should take part in it on time. Please wear your sports shoes. Don't be late!
> 　　　　　　　　　　The Students' Union

## 七、批改与评分要点

### 7.1 本年级评分看什么

| 评分项 | 占比 | 说明 |
|--------|------|------|
| 篇章结构 | 30% | 有开头—经过—结尾，分段清楚，应用文格式正确 |
| 内容要点 | 25% | 紧扣题目，要点齐全，无遗漏 |
| 时态语法 | 25% | 时态前后一致，主谓一致，比较级正确 |
| 连贯衔接 | 10% | 恰当使用 first/then/because/when/if |
| 词汇拼写 | 10% | 词块丰富，大小写、标点、拼写规范 |

### 7.2 批改三原则

1. **先表扬后纠错**：先肯定亮点（用对了时态、词块），再指出问题，保护写作信心。
2. **一次抓 1—2 类**：六年级重点抓"时态一致"和"篇章结构"，不要满篇红。
3. **抓高频错误**：时态混用、过去式/过去分词错、比较级 more 与 -er 重复、应用文格式缺落款。

### 7.3 写作"五查"口诀（六年级升级版）

> 一查时态是否一致 → 二查主谓单复数 → 三查复合句连词用对没 → 四查大小写标点 → 五查篇章是否有头有尾、紧扣主题

### 7.4 小升初写作提示

1. **审题先圈关键词**：圈出话题、时态信号词（last/tomorrow/ever）、人称。
2. **先列提纲再下笔**：开头一句、经过三句、结尾一句，凑够 6 句以上。
3. **用上得分词块**：每篇争取用 1—2 个连词复合句 + 1 个观点句（I think…）。
4. **应用文先对格式**：日记看日期天气，书信看 Dear/Yours，便条看落款，通知看标题。
5. **写完一定回读**：按"五查口诀"逐项检查，时态一致最关键。

---

> **结语**：六年级是小学写作的收官与小升初的起点。坚持"四阶段"打基础——先写对词、再写对句、然后连成段、最后成篇成文。多用高频词块，写完必回读，写作能力一定稳步提升。加油！
